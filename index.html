Patch Notes

Paytable

Multipliers shown × bet ÷ 25 lines. Wins pay LEFT-to-RIGHT on 25 fixed lines. EYE = WILD (substitutes all except SCARAB). SCARAB = SCATTER, triggers TOMB OF RICHES.

Expanding Wild

EYE OF HORUS on reels 2, 3, or 4 expands to fill the whole reel before paylines pay.

Tomb of Riches

3+ SCARAB scatters trigger free spins:

  • 3 = 8 spins, 4 = 12, 5 = 18
  • EYE wilds become STICKY (locked expanded for the rest of the bonus, up to 3 reels)
  • 2+ scatters during bonus → +5 spins
